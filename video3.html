Science, Technology, and Society: Interview

In our interview about how students use technology, most of them shared that they’re very familiar with the latest tools and they use them almost every day. Some even said they feel like they can’t go a day without it, especially for school stuff like research, notes, or group chats. They kinda depend on it a lot, not just for school, but for everyday life too. To them, tech isn’t just helpful, it’s something they feel they really need to get through the day.
