Readings in Philippine History: Interview

For this interview-video, we asked students from different courses—Engineering, Criminology, Education, Psychology, and IT—how familiar they are with the 1987 Philippine Constitution. We wanted to know what they actually know about it, which part they value the most, and how important it is for them, as students, to understand it. We also asked how the internet or social media helped them learn about their rights, and if they could change or add something to the Constitution, what would it be and why. It was interesting hearing their honest thoughts. Some were confident, others admitted they still have a lot to learn. But many agreed that knowing our Constitution is really important, especially in today’s time.
