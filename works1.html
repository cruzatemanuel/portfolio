Graduation Ceremony: Presentation

Creating the our high school graduation ceremony presentation was a mix of pressure and pride. I focused on getting everything right from the order of the slides to the photos and names, because I knew how important this day was for everyone. It wasn’t about making it flashy, just clear, organized, and meaningful. I felt responsible for making sure things ran smoothly and that each part of the presentation added to the moment. Being part of that behind-the-scenes work made me feel proud to contribute to something so important for all of us.
