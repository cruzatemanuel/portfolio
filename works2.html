Senior High School: Research

Working on our research and prototype paper, "Developing an Arduino-based Car Parking System for Bauan Technical Integrated High School Senior High School Teachers," gave us a practical experience in applying technology to solve real-life problems. We designed and tested a smart parking system using Arduino, focusing on how it could help our teachers park more efficiently on campus. Through surveys and interviews, we learned how they interacted with the system, what worked well, and what didn’t. While some faced issues with navigation and minor bugs, most teachers appreciated the concept and saw its usefulness. The feedback helped us recognize what to improve and how to make it more user-friendly. Overall, the project taught us how important user experience is in system development, especially when creating something meant to serve the school community.

Type: Academic | Year: 2024 | Author: Cruzat, Faltado, Marasigan, and Sarsonas
