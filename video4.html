Science, Technology, and Society: Final Project

For our final project in STS and Computer Programming, we created a video that introduces our prototype fare tracking system designed to make commuting more transparent and efficient. To keep it engaging, we opened with a skit-vlog style segment where we asked fellow students to share their jeepney experiences through an anonymous NGL link. I read out their stories on-camera, blending humor with relatable frustrations to set the tone. The goal was to mix fun with curiosity, using those real voices to highlight why this kind of system matters and how it could make a difference.
