A FILM FOR LIFE AND WORKS OF RIZAL: La Pluma Del Patriota

Working on La Pluma Del Patriota was one of the most meaningful projects I’ve been part of back in my First Year - First Semester. I wrote the script, so I spent a lot of time getting to know Rizal’s life and his big historical moments. I was also the assistant videographer, which meant I got to help bring those words to life on camera. Being involved in both writing and filming gave me a connection to the story and it reminded me why telling these historical stories really matters.

Type: Academic | Year: 2024 | Role: Scriptwriter, Assistant Videographer
